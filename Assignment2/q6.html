Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 clove Garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fresh Basil (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and saut√© the garlic for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook for another 3-4 minutes until soft.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the tomatoes and garlic.
  5. Toss everything together and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
